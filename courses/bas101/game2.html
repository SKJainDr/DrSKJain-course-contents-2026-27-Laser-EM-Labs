🎮 Relativity Speed Lab

A mystery spacecraft is travelling at a hidden speed v (as a fraction of light speed, v/c). All you're told is what its clock and metre-stick would look like from Earth — the dilated time and contracted length. Adjust your own spacecraft's speed until your numbers match the mystery ship's, then submit your estimate of v/c.
