🎮 Population Inversion Simulator

Choose a pumping scheme (2-level, 3-level, or 4-level) and increase the pump rate. Watch the level populations evolve — only the 3-level and 4-level schemes can reach population inversion (N₂ > N₁), the condition needed for laser gain. This is a simplified rate-equation model of the physics in Unit 1.
